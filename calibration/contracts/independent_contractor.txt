INDEPENDENT CONTRACTOR AGREEMENT

This Agreement is between Northwind Inc. ("Company") and the contractor ("Contractor") for
ongoing marketing and design services.

1. SERVICES AND CHANGES
1.1 Contractor will provide services as directed by Company.
1.2 Company may amend the scope, rates, and terms of this Agreement at any time upon written
notice to Contractor, and continued performance constitutes acceptance.

2. COMPENSATION
2.1 Company shall pay undisputed invoices within sixty (60) days.
2.2 If this Agreement is terminated, Company shall have no obligation to pay for work in
progress that has not been delivered and accepted.

3. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
3.1 All deliverables and all intellectual property Contractor creates, conceives, or reduces
to practice during the term — whether or not related to the services — are works made for
hire and the property of Company. Contractor waives all moral rights.

4. NON-COMPETE AND EXCLUSIVITY
4.1 During the term and for eighteen (18) months after, Contractor shall not perform similar
services for any other company in Company's industry.

5. INDEMNIFICATION
5.1 Contractor shall indemnify, defend, and hold Company harmless from any claims arising
from the services, including claims caused by Company's own instructions.

6. TERMINATION
6.1 Company may terminate immediately at any time. Contractor must provide sixty (60) days'
notice and forfeits any outstanding fees if Contractor terminates early.

7. GENERAL
This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Texas.
