MUTUAL NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT

This Mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between the parties to
facilitate discussion of a potential business relationship.

1. CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION
1.1 "Confidential Information" means any and all information disclosed by either party,
whether or not marked as confidential, including any information the receiving party should
reasonably understand to be confidential, and including all notes, analyses, and derivative
materials. There are no exclusions for information that is or becomes public.

2. OBLIGATIONS
2.1 The receiving party shall hold all Confidential Information in strict confidence in
perpetuity, with no expiration of these obligations.
2.2 Upon request, the receiving party shall not be required to return or destroy Confidential
Information and may retain copies indefinitely.

3. NON-SOLICITATION
3.1 During the term and for three (3) years after, neither party shall solicit or hire any
employee or contractor of the other party.

4. FEEDBACK AND RESIDUALS
4.1 Any feedback, ideas, or suggestions provided by the disclosing party shall become the
property of the receiving party, which may use them for any purpose without compensation.

5. REMEDIES
5.1 In the event of any breach, the breaching party shall pay all of the other party's
attorneys' fees and shall be liable for liquidated damages of $50,000 per breach.

6. GENERAL
This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of New York.
