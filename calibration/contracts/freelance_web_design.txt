WEB DESIGN & DEVELOPMENT SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Agreement is entered into between Acme Holdings LLC ("Client") and the undersigned
contractor ("Provider") for website design and development services.

Provider shall design and develop a marketing website per the specifications in Exhibit A.
Client may request reasonable revisions and additions during the project at no additional
charge to ensure Client satisfaction.

2.2 Client shall pay all invoices within ninety (90) days of receipt.
2.3 Client shall withhold twenty percent (20%) of the total fee as a final payment, payable
only after Client confirms, in its sole discretion, that the website is fully satisfactory.

4.1 Provider warrants the work will be free of defects.
4.2 Provider shall indemnify and hold Client harmless from any and all claims, damages, and
losses arising out of the services. Provider's liability under this Agreement shall be
unlimited.

5. NON-COMPETITION
5.1 During the term and for a period of two (2) years thereafter, Provider shall not provide
web design or development services to any business that competes with Client or operates in
the same industry as Client.

6. TERMINATION
6.1 Client may terminate this Agreement at any time, for any reason, with no further payment
obligation beyond work accepted in writing as of the termination date.
